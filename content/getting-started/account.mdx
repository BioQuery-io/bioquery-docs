# Creating an Account

BioQuery offers free accounts with limited queries, and expanded access for researchers.

## Why Create an Account?

- **Save Query History** - Access your past queries from any device
- **Share Results** - Generate permalinks to share with collaborators
- **Higher Limits** - Free accounts get 2 queries/day (vs. example-only for anonymous users)

## Sign Up Options

### Google Sign-In (Recommended)

1. Click **Sign In** or **Get Started**
2. Select **Continue with Google**
3. Authorize BioQuery to access your basic profile info
4. You're in!

### Email Sign-Up

1. Click **Sign In** or **Get Started**
2. Select **Sign up with Email**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for a magic link
5. Click the link to complete registration

## Account Tiers

| Feature | Anonymous | Free Account | Researcher |
|---------|-----------|--------------|------------|
| View example queries | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Run new queries | ❌ | 2/day | Unlimited |
| Save history | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Share results | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| API access | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

## Request Researcher Access

If you need unlimited queries for your research:

1. [Contact us](mailto:hello@bioquery.io) with your institutional email
2. Briefly describe your research use case
3. We'll upgrade your account within 24 hours

<Callout>
  BioQuery is free for academic research. We're committed to supporting the cancer research community.
</Callout>

## Managing Your Account

### View Account Settings

1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. Select **Settings**

### Sign Out

1. Click your profile icon
2. Select **Sign Out**

### Delete Account

To delete your account and all associated data, email [hello@bioquery.io](mailto:hello@bioquery.io).
