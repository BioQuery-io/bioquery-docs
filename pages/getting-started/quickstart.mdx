# Quick Start

Get started with BioQuery in under 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Navigate to BioQuery

Go to [bioquery.io/query](https://bioquery.io/query) in your browser.

## Step 2: Sign In (Optional)

While you can view example queries without signing in, you'll need an account to run new queries.

- Click **Sign In** or **Get Started**
- Sign in with Google or create an account with email

## Step 3: Ask a Question

Type a natural language question about cancer genomics. For example:

```
Is DDR1 expression higher in papillary RCC compared to clear cell RCC?
```

Or try clicking one of the example queries on the page.

## Step 4: Review Your Results

After a few seconds, you'll see a **Query Card** with:

1. **Natural Language Answer** - A plain English summary of the findings
2. **Interactive Figure** - Boxplot, Kaplan-Meier curve, or bar chart
3. **Statistical Results** - P-value, effect size, sample sizes
4. **Methods** - Grant-ready text you can copy

## Step 5: Export Your Results

Click **Export** to download:

- **PNG** - High-resolution image for presentations
- **SVG** - Vector format for editing in Illustrator
- **JSON** - Full card data for programmatic use

## Step 6: Share Your Results

Click **Share** to copy a permalink. Anyone with the link can view your Query Card.
